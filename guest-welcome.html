Preparing for the Interview

 
We are grateful to have you as a guest on BizDayGlobal! We strive to make our guests look good. To that end, please read through the following guidelines and let us know if you have questions.
 
* Wear solid colors- Just like in TV news, it's always best to wear solid colors.
 
* Make eye contact- Position your computer or phone at eye level and look at your host or directly into the camera. When in doubt, look into the camera.
 
* Keep water nearby- To avoid interruptions, always keep a glass of water nearby in case you need to hydrate mid-interview.
 
* Arrive early- Join the stream 5-10 minutes early for testing.
 
* Setup with plenty of light on your face- use natural light if possible.
 
* Keep your head center framed, with a small amount of room between the top of your head, and the top of the frame.
 
* Stay close to the computer so we can hear you â€“ the distance you need to type comfortably at the keyboard is perfect.

 
 

Technology

 
* Interviews are conducted via Zoom. Do you want a custom background to appear behind you during the interview?
 
* If you can, hardwire your computer into the ethernet port on your router.
 
* Close all browser windows, and limit any unnecessary bandwidth usage on your network (streaming, downloads, etc).
 
* Use Google Chrome if possible for a stronger, faster connection.
 
* We recommend using a headset with a microphone if possible.
 
* Make sure your camera and mic are open when you join.
 
* Eliminate background noise- ensure phone and chat services are muted.
 
 
 
Again, thank you for appearing on BizDayGlobal. Feel free to contact any member of our team if you have questions.
